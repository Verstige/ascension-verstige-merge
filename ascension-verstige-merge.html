03 · THE FRAMEWORK

Wale's methodology, cleanly mapped.

Six core constructs. Each one has a clear definition, a clear purpose, and a clear place in the scanner output. This is what we're pulling into Verstige OS.

  • Quarter Levels
    Institutional price zones at the .00, .25, .50, .75 of every pair. Price is pulled to them, swept at them, and reversed from them — consistently, repeatedly, every session.
  • 5-Stage Lifecycle
    Not In Play → Watch → Developing → Confirmed → Entry Ready. State, not signal. Each state tells you exactly what to do (or not do).
  • 6-Condition Score
    0 to 6 institutional confirmations per setup. 4+/6 = High Probability. 6/6 = Institutional Grade (A+). Score appears as a grade badge on every card.
  • Macro Context
    DXY for USD pairs, synthetic Yen Basket for JPY crosses. One-word verdict: Aligned / Conflicting / Neutral. Drives whether the setup is real or noise.
  • Entry Latch
    Once a setup reaches Confirmed, the plan is frozen. Entry, SL, TP1/TP2/TP3 all locked. No second-guessing, no moving targets. The plan is the plan until the trade resolves.
  • 2% Risk Formula
    Account × 2% = max risk per trade. Max risk ÷ (SL pips × pip value) = lot size. Every trade, no exceptions. The math that keeps you in the game.
